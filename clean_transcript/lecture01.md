Large language models and generative AI are transforming everything around us:

## Historical Perspective: Early Chatbots

Let’s look at how early systems behaved.

One of the first chatbots developed was **ELIZA**, designed to act like a therapist.

Example interaction:

- User asks for help learning large language models  
- ELIZA responds with generic, irrelevant questions  
- The conversation quickly becomes meaningless  

These systems were:

- Rule-based  
- Not context-aware  
- Not capable of real understanding

## Open Source vs Closed Source Models

There are two types of LLM ecosystems:

### Closed Source (e.g., GPT-4)
- Internal architecture not publicly available  
- Access via APIs  

### Open Source (e.g., LLaMA 3.1)
- Architecture and weights available  
- Increasingly competitive performance  

Trend:
- The performance gap between open-source and closed-source models is shrinking
